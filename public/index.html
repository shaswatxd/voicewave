Getting Started

Three steps. No account.

From nothing to talking with friends in under 10 seconds.

01

Create a room

Pick a display name, optionally set a password, hit Create. You get a 6-character room code instantly — no email, no verification.
02

Share the link or code

Send the invite link or room code to whoever you want in the room. They join straight from their browser — nothing to install.
03

Talk, share, chat

Voice connects peer-to-peer for low latency. Go live with your screen, drop messages in chat, or fire off the soundboard.
Capabilities

Everything you need in a room

Built to feel like Discord, without the account or the download.

🎙️ Voice 🖥️ Screen Share 💬 Chat 🛡️ Moderation 🎨 Customization
🎙️
Peer-to-Peer Voice
Direct WebRTC connections for ultra-low latency — your voice travels straight to other participants, with noise gate and echo cancellation built in.
🖥️
Multi-Stream Screen Share
Discord-style screen sharing — several people can go live at once, up to 1440p/60fps, with adjustable quality and optional system audio.
🖥️ Desktop app & desktop browser · not on mobile
💬
Rich Text Chat
Markdown, @mentions with autocomplete, reactions, replies, message editing, file sharing, and live typing indicators.
🔒
Password-Protected Rooms
Lock a room down with a password, generate invite links with expiry, and lock or ban to control exactly who gets in.
🛡️
Admin & Moderation
Kick, mute, force-mute, transfer host, assign moderators, tune room permissions, and ban — full Discord-style room control.
🎵
Built-in Soundboard
10 synthesized sound effects — airhorn, clap, laugh, ding, and more — generated on the fly, no audio files to load.
🎛️
Fine-Grained Audio Controls
Per-user volume, master volume, mic gain, noise gate, push-to-talk, and a low-bandwidth mode for weaker connections.
📊
Polls & Pinned Messages
Spin up a poll for group decisions, pin important messages to the top, and react with the full emoji picker.
👤
Custom Profiles
Upload an avatar, pick an accent color, set a display name and status — your identity carries across sessions on this device.
🎨
Themes & Wallpapers
Dark and light modes with four wallpaper presets — Cosmic, Obsidian, Forest, and Glass — so long sessions stay easy on the eyes.
⌨️
Keyboard Shortcuts
M to mute, D to deafen, C for chat, Space to push-to-talk, @ to mention someone — built for keeping your hands on the keyboard.
📱
Works Everywhere
Install as a PWA, run the Electron desktop app with system tray support, or just open it in a browser tab. Your call.

Persistent JSON Rooms
Voice and screen share connect directly between participants (WebRTC mesh) instead of routing through a media server — lower latency, but each sharer's upload scales with how many people are actually watching them. Fine for the typical small room; very large rooms with several simultaneous screen shares will feel the difference.
