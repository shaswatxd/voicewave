Capabilities

Everything You Need

🎙️
Peer-to-Peer Voice
Direct WebRTC connections for ultra-low latency. No server relay — your voice travels directly to other participants.
🔒
Password Protected
Create private rooms with passwords. Only people with the password can join your conversation.
💬
Text Chat
Built-in messaging with emoji support, file sharing, and typing indicators alongside voice.
🎵
Soundboard
10 built-in sounds to add fun to your conversations. Airhorn, clap, laugh, and more.
🎛️
Audio Controls
Per-user volume, master volume, mic gain, noise gate, and professional audio processing.
📱
Works Everywhere
PWA support, Electron desktop app, or just use your browser. No downloads required.
